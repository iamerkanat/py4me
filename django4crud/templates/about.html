Project Management by Dilnaz

As the developer behind our university CRUD project, I carefully selected the tools and technologies to ensure the project's success. For the backend, I chose Django for its robustness, scalability, and rapid development capabilities.

Django's batteries-included approach provides a wealth of built-in features, including authentication, ORM, and admin panel, allowing me to focus more on building the core functionality of the application rather than reinventing the wheel.

Moreover, Django's strong community support and extensive documentation make it an ideal choice for projects of any size, ensuring long-term maintainability and stability.

Respecting Tradition with Bootstrap

For the frontend, I chose Bootstrap out of respect for its legacy as a pioneering CSS framework. Bootstrap's intuitive grid system, pre-styled components, and responsive design principles allowed me to create a visually appealing and user-friendly interface with ease.

While newer CSS frameworks have emerged, I have a deep admiration for Bootstrap's simplicity and reliability. By leveraging Bootstrap, I was able to ensure cross-browser compatibility and consistent styling throughout the project.
